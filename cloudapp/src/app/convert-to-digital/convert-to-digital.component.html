A swift way of converting a borrowing request, with a matching local ressource to digitization request
...you need to:

1. Navigate to "Borrowing Requets" page in Alma.
2. Select the Borrowing Request in the cloud app selectbox below.
3. Find an appropriate item to digitize. By the time a sigle item is selected (view- or edit-mode), the "Convert to digitization Request"-button will appear.
4. Click "Convert to digitization Request".
5. Please notice any alertmessages in the cloudApp indicating that an error has orcurred.
